Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

StoryBot ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal video publishing tool that automates the creation and posting of short-form video content to social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

Information We Collect

StoryBot collects only the minimum information required to publish content on your behalf:

  • Authentication tokens — OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens provided by social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) when you authorize the app. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Video content — Videos you create using the app, stored locally on your device until uploaded.
  • Performance data — View and like counts you manually enter, stored locally for content optimization.

How We Use Your Information

Authentication tokens are used solely to publish videos to your authorized social media accounts. We do not use your information for any other purpose.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your personal computer. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. No data is transmitted to any third party other than the social media platforms you explicitly authorize.

Third-Party Services

StoryBot interacts with the following third-party APIs on your behalf:

  • TikTok Content Posting API
  • Instagram Graph API
  • YouTube Data API v3
  • OpenAI API (for text-to-speech and transcription)
  • Anthropic API (for content generation)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone. Period.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by removing the application files from your computer. To revoke platform access, visit the settings page of each social media platform.

Children's Privacy

StoryBot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the contact information on our social media profiles.
