StoryBot

AI-powered short-form video creation and publishing platform for content creators.

What is StoryBot?

StoryBot is a content creation platform that helps creators produce engaging short-form story videos for social media. It uses AI to write compelling scripts, generate professional narration, add animated captions, and publish directly to platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Features

  • AI Script Writing — Generates engaging story scripts with strong hooks and pacing optimized for short-form video
  • Text-to-Speech — Professional narration using natural-sounding AI voices
  • Animated Captions — Word-by-word karaoke-style captions synced to audio for maximum viewer retention
  • Multi-Platform Publishing — Direct posting to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts with platform-optimized metadata
  • Quality Assurance — AI agents review scripts and finished videos before publishing to ensure content quality
  • Performance Analytics — Tracks video performance to improve future content recommendations

How It Works

  1. StoryBot discovers trending story topics and generates a video script
  2. AI agents review and refine the script for maximum engagement
  3. The platform renders the video with narration, background footage, and animated captions
  4. A quality check agent reviews the final video
  5. The creator reviews and approves the content
  6. StoryBot publishes to connected social media accounts with optimized titles, captions, and hashtags
